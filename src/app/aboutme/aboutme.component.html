About me

As a german-speaking front-end developer, I have initial project experience in building web applications with JavaScript, Typescript and Angular. My interest in programming arose from my enthusiasm for reprogramming applications. As a result, I decided to pursue further training in the IT sector. In the last few months I have been able to implement various projects independently and together in small agile teams. My portfolio includes some of my projects that highlight my programming skills.

My experience includes developing landing pages, a range of web applications including business apps and object-oriented “jump and run” games.

As a former educator, I am used to taking responsibility and working in a structured and conscientious manner. I bring these characteristics into web development. For example in team projects with other developers.
